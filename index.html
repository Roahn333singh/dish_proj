Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • Boneless-Chicken(500gm)
  • Tomatoes(6-medium)
  • Onions(5-medium)
  • Butter
  • Amul-Cream
  • Kashmiri-Mirch
  • Kasturi-Methi
  • Honey

Recipe

  1. Boil chicken piece in lemon water
  2. Take out tomato pure and stire fry with onions and Ginger Garlic paste
  3. Cool it and grind it in mixer to make fine paste
  4. Add some butter to it and cook it for 5 min ,add some salt and cream
  5. Add Kashmiri-Mirch and Kasturi-Methi
  6. Now add boiled chicken along with some honey to give some sweet flavour to it
  7. Your butter chicken is now ready !! Serve it with chappatis

Poha

Ingredients

  • Flattened rice 250gm
  • Tomatoes(6-medium)
  • Onions(5-medium)
  • Butter

Recipe

  1. Take Flattened Rice and sink it in water for 5min
  2. Take out tomato pure and stire fry with onions and Ginger Garlic paste
  3. Cool it and grind it in mixer to make fine paste
